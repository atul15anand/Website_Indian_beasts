Protein shakes are as important in being fit as to hoader a stick.They form a major part of protein in take of the day.Besides healthy,usually they taste really well(Ya some exceptions are there!!).
Basically their are two categories of them:
  1. Pre-Workout
  2. Post-Workout
The only difference is the carbs content.Post workout have a bit higher content as your body's glycogen stores are used as fuel during exercise,and thus get depleted. Thereby consuming carbs after your workout helps replenish them.Consuming 1.1–1.5 grams/kg of body weight within 30 minutes after training results in proper glycogen resynthesis.The recipes I am gonna share are both with and without protein powder coz not everybody can afford or want to buy.
You are free to customise a bit of all these.All of these are ones that I have personally tasted.

Peanut-Oatmeal Protein shake:

  • 30 gms. oats(approx.)
  • Peanut butter 2 tbsp
  • Skimmed Milk 250ml(depends on you)
  • Honey 1-2 tspn
Macros:
Carbs:38 gm , Protein:20gm , Fats:19 gm (omega-3 especially)

A harsh truth about not using whey protein is you will always find it difficult to achieve protein intake lest you are non-vegan.

A very good source of protein is Yogurt.You can make it very easily at home also.

Here is the procedure:
  • Heat the milk to about 106-114°F
  • Add the bacterial culture(Yeast or 'khamir')
  • Leave to ferment overnight.(basically its form of curd)
Now you can get various flavours just by adding one or the other thing in the above recipe(like vanilla,chocolate,strwberry) Its better form is what is called Greek Yogurt(Lesser fat and double the protein content). It is just concentrated form less water.

Banana Peanut Butter:

  • 2 bananas (variable quantity)
  • Almonds 10-12
  • Roasted chickpeas(20 gms)
  • Milk low fat(250ml)
  • Honey 1-2 tspn
Macros:
Carbs:55 gm , Protein:18gm , Fats:7 gm

Strawberry Protein Shakes:

  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 5 strawberries ,stalks removed
  • milk 350ml
  • Vanilla extract(optional)
  • Water (as req.)
  • Sugar (or as Roohafja)
Macros:
Carbs:25 gm , Protein:36gm , Fats:5 gm (omega-3 especially)

Coffee Protein Shake:

  • Milk 400ml
  • Coffee 2 shashe
  • Almonds 10-12
  • Roasted chana
  • Sugar to taste
Macros:
Carbs:38 gm , Protein:20gm , Fats:19 gm

Chocolate Protein Shake:
